Как рассчитываются уровни в Sunrise Program?

Your level depends on 2 parametres.

  • 1st parametre — Your personal investments.
  • 2d parametre — The sum of your personal investments and the investments of the investors invited by you.
    For getting the level, both parametres should fit the table or be higher. The level is automatically displayed in the personal account once balance is refilled for necessary sums.
